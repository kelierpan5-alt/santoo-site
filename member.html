Pan Yao

Visual Artist / Independent Researcher

Pan Yao is a visual artist with over 20 years of continuous practice across visual art, sound, and writing. His work focuses on the construction of visual systems that explore structural relationships within perception.

He has participated in international exhibitions in France, South Korea, and Japan, and received the Special Selection Award at the Kyoto International Exhibition (2023). His writing has been featured by Paper Republic (UK).

His recent work develops an ongoing visual framework that examines stability, tension, and transformation within structured visual fields. This project is continuously expanded through visual production, text, and cross-media experimentation.
